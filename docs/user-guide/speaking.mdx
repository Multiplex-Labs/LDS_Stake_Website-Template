## What This Page Does

The Speaking Schedule page (`/leader/speaking`) provides a central view of who is speaking at each ward on each date. Stake leaders and clerks can add, edit, and remove assignments from this page.

## Viewing the Schedule

The schedule is displayed as a table or calendar view. Each entry shows:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Date | The Sunday (or other date) of the speaking assignment |
| Ward | The ward where the speaker will speak |
| Speaker | The name of the speaker |
| Topic | The topic assigned to the speaker (optional) |

You can scroll through past and future dates. Use the ward filter (if present) to narrow the view to a specific ward.

## Adding a Speaking Assignment

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Add dialog">
    Click the **Add Assignment** button (or the **+** icon) at the top of the page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a date">
    Use the date picker to choose the date of the speaking assignment. Future dates are most common, but past dates can be recorded as well.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a ward">
    Choose the ward where the speaker will be speaking. All system wards are available in the dropdown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the speaker's name">
    Type the speaker's name. You may also be able to select from the list of portal users if the speaker has a portal account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter a topic (optional)">
    Type the talk topic in the topic field. This field is optional — if you leave it blank, the assignment still saves and appears in the schedule without a topic.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. The assignment appears in the schedule immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The topic field is entirely optional. It is common to add the assignment first and fill in the topic once it has been confirmed with the speaker.
</Note>

## Editing an Assignment

Click on an existing assignment row or its **Edit** button to open the edit dialog. All fields (date, ward, speaker name, topic) can be updated. Click **Save** to apply changes.

## Removing an Assignment

Click the **Delete** or **Remove** button on an assignment row and confirm the prompt. The assignment is removed from the schedule immediately.
