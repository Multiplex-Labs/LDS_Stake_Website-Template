The **High Council Speaking Schedule** page (`/leader/speaking`) is a read-only calendar that shows which High Councilor is assigned to speak at which ward each month, along with the monthly topic. Managing assignments and topics is done from the [Admin Hub](/user-guide/admin) at `/leader/admin?tab=speaking`.

<Callout type="info">
  Viewing the speaking schedule requires an authenticated leader account. Managing assignments requires the **MANAGE_SPEAKING_SCHEDULE** permission.
</Callout>

---

## Viewing the Speaking Schedule

Navigate to `/leader/speaking`. The page heading reads **High Council Speaking Schedule**. A toggle in the top-right corner switches between **Month View** and **Full Year**.

### Month View

**Month View** is the default. It shows assignments for a single month at a time.

**Navigating by month**

Use the **left chevron** (`‹`) and **right chevron** (`›`) buttons next to the current month and year label (for example, `JUNE 2026`) to move backward or forward one month. When you navigate past December the year increments automatically; navigating before January decrements it.

**Topic card**

If a topic has been set for the current month, a card appears above the assignment grid displaying:

- The topic text as a large heading.
- The reference material (scripture or other source) below the topic, preceded by a book icon.

If no topic has been set, the card shows **No topic set**.

**Assignment cards**

Below the topic card, the page renders a responsive grid of cards — one card per High Councilor who has a ward assignment that month. Each card shows:

- A **ward badge** identifying the ward (for example, `3rd Ward`).
- The **High Councilor's full name**.

Cards are sorted by ward. If no assignments exist for the selected month, a placeholder message reads **No speaking assignments for this month.**

**Not Speaking This Month**

When at least one High Councilor has an assignment, a section labeled **Not Speaking This Month** appears below the grid. It lists, as outline badges, the names of all High Councilors who have no ward assignment for that month.

### Full Year View

Click **Full Year** to switch to the year view. This view displays a scrollable table where:

| Axis | Content |
|------|---------|
| Rows | One row per High Councilor |
| Columns | Twelve month columns (Jan – Dec), abbreviated |

Each cell shows the **ward number** (for example, `3`) if that High Councilor has an assignment for that month, or a dim dot (`•`) if they are unassigned. The **High Councilor** column is sticky so names remain visible when scrolling horizontally.

<Callout type="info">
  The year displayed in Full Year view is the same year that was active when you were last in Month View. To view a different year, switch to Month View, navigate to a month in the desired year, and then switch back to Full Year.
</Callout>

## Managing the Speaking Schedule (Admin Hub)

Navigate to `/leader/admin?tab=speaking` to access the speaking management tools. This tab contains two sections: **Speaking Topics** and **Ward Schedule**.

<Callout type="warn">
  Both sections require the **MANAGE_SPEAKING_SCHEDULE** permission. If the tab is not visible, contact your stake clerk or system administrator.
</Callout>

### Year Navigation

Both sections share the same year selector. Use the **left chevron** and **right chevron** buttons flanking the year dropdown, or click the dropdown directly to jump to a specific year. The available range spans two years before and two years after the current year. Changing the year reloads both sections simultaneously.

---

### Speaking Topics

The **Speaking Topics** section lists all twelve months in a grid with the following columns:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Month** | A colored status circle with the abbreviated month name, the full month name, and a status subtext |
| **Topic** | A text input for the speaking topic |
| **Reference Material** | A text input for a scripture or other reference source |
| **Status** | A badge reflecting the current state of the row |
| *(action)* | A **Save** or **Edit** button |

**Topic status values**

| Status | Badge color | Meaning |
|--------|-------------|---------|
| Planned | Green | A topic has been saved for this month. Inputs are locked. |
| Unsaved | Amber | The topic has been edited locally but not yet saved. |
| Empty | Red | No topic has been set or edited for this month. |

**Adding or editing a topic**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate the month row">
    Find the row for the month you want to update. An **Empty** row has no topic yet; a **Planned** row has a saved topic.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Unlock the inputs if the row is Planned">
    If the row status is **Planned**, the inputs are disabled. Click the **Edit** button on the right side of the row to unlock them. The status changes to **Unsaved**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the topic and reference material">
    Type the speaking topic in the **Topic** field (placeholder: *Add topic…*). Optionally enter a scripture passage or other source in the **Reference Material** field (placeholder: *Add reference material…*).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the row">
    Click **Save** on the right side of the row. The status badge changes to **Planned** and the inputs lock again. A **Topic saved.** toast confirms success.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
  The **Save** button is disabled until the **Topic** field contains at least one non-whitespace character. The Reference Material field is optional.
</Callout>

**Saving all unsaved topics at once**

If you have edited topics across multiple months without saving each individually, click **Save All Changes** in the bottom-right corner of the section. This submits all rows currently in **Unsaved** status in a single operation. An **All changes saved.** toast confirms success. If any individual save fails, the failed rows remain in **Unsaved** state and an error toast reports the count.

---

### Ward Schedule

The **Ward Schedule** section displays a table for assigning High Councilors to wards by month.

**Table layout**

| Axis | Content |
|------|---------|
| Rows | One row per High Councilor (with avatar and name, sticky left column) |
| Columns | Twelve month columns (Jan – Dec, abbreviated) plus a trailing actions column |

Each cell in the grid represents one High Councilor for one month. A colored badge showing the ward number (for example, `5`) appears when an assignment exists; a dim dash (`—`) appears when no ward is assigned.

**Assigning or changing a ward for a cell**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click the cell">
    Click any cell in the schedule grid. The cell activates and a dropdown opens immediately, showing all wards in the stake plus a **— None** option at the top.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a ward">
    Choose a ward from the list. The dropdown closes and the assignment is saved automatically. A **Schedule updated.** toast confirms success.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remove an assignment">
    To clear an existing assignment, click the cell and select **— None** from the top of the dropdown. The cell reverts to a dim dash.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Clearing an entire month**

Each month column header includes a small **X** button beneath the abbreviated month label. Clicking it opens a confirmation dialog:

> **Clear all [Month] assignments?**
> This will remove every ward assignment for [Month] [Year]. This cannot be undone.

Click **Clear** to proceed or **Cancel** to abort. A **Month cleared.** toast confirms success.

**Clearing all assignments for a High Councilor**

Each High Councilor row has a **trash icon** button on the far right. Clicking it opens a confirmation dialog:

> **Clear all assignments for [Name]?**
> This will remove every ward assignment for [Name] in [Year]. This cannot be undone.

Click **Clear** to proceed or **Cancel** to abort. A **Schedule cleared.** toast confirms success.

<Callout type="warn">
  Clearing a month or a High Councilor's assignments for the year cannot be undone from within the portal. Assignments must be re-entered manually afterward.
</Callout>
