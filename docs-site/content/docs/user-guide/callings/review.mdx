title: "Review Calling Proposals"
description: "Approve or deny calling proposals as a Stake Presidency or High Council member from the Review page."
---

The Review page (`/leader/callings/review`) is where Stake Presidency and High Council members cast approval votes on calling proposals. Viewing requires the `VIEW_CALLING_PROPOSALS` permission; voting additionally requires holding a qualifying calling.

<Callout type="note">
  Release proposals do not appear on this page. They skip approval stages and enter the Kanban board at the Interview stage.
</Callout>

## What Appears Here

The Review page contains two independent sections:

- **Stake Presidency Review** — proposals currently in the **SP Approval** stage (stage 0)
- **High Council Review** — proposals currently in the **HC Approval** stage (stage 1)

Each section renders a table with columns: Name, Calling/Assignment, Ward, Approvals, and Date Submitted.

<Callout type="note">
  Release proposals do not appear on this page. Releases skip both approval stages and enter the pipeline at the Interview stage, so they are managed directly on the [Manage (Kanban)](/user-guide/callings/manage) board.
</Callout>

Rows where you have already submitted a vote are visually dimmed, and the Approvals column shows a **Voted** badge in place of the approval count.

## Opening a Proposal

Click any row to open the proposal dialog. The dialog contains two tabs.

### Details tab

Shows three information cards:

- **Candidate** — member's first name, last name, and spouse name (if provided), and their ward
- **Recommendation** — the proposed calling, the proposal type (New Calling or Release), and the submission date
- **Review Status** — the current approval stage, a live count of approvals and denials from eligible reviewers, and your voting access (Can vote / Voting only / Already voted)

If you do not hold the calling required to vote at the current stage, a notice in the Review Status card explains that you are viewing the proposal in read-only mode.

### Votes tab

Shows every eligible reviewer for the current stage with their vote status:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Approved** (green badge) | This reviewer submitted an approval |
| **Denied** (red badge) | This reviewer submitted a denial |
| **Pending** (outline badge) | This reviewer has not yet voted |

The tab header badge shows the total number of votes submitted so far.

## Who Can Vote

Voting rights are determined by your portal calling assignment, not by your permission level alone.

| Stage | Eligible voters |
|-------|----------------|
| Stake Presidency Review | Users assigned to the **Stake President**, **Stake First Counselor**, or **Stake Second Counselor** calling |
| High Council Review | Users assigned to the **High Councilor** calling |

If you have the `VIEW_CALLING_PROPOSALS` permission but do not hold one of the above callings for the stage you are viewing, the Approve and Deny buttons will not appear and the dialog will note that you are viewing only.

## Approving or Denying a Proposal

When you are an eligible voter and have not yet voted on a proposal, the dialog footer shows two buttons:

- **Deny** — records your vote as a denial
- **Approve** — records your vote as an approval

Click the appropriate button. The dialog closes, the board data refreshes, and a toast notification confirms your action.

<Callout type="warn">
  Votes are final in the UI. Once you submit either Approve or Deny, both buttons are disabled for that proposal and cannot be changed from this page. If a vote was submitted in error, contact your clerk or system administrator.
</Callout>

## The Approval Threshold

Each approval stage has a configurable threshold. When the number of approvals for a proposal reaches the threshold, the system **automatically advances** the proposal to the next stage.

| Stage | Threshold environment variable | Default |
|-------|-------------------------------|---------|
| SP Approval | `SP_APPROVAL_THRESHOLD` | 2 |
| HC Approval | `HC_APPROVAL_THRESHOLD` | 3 |

When a proposal auto-advances, it disappears from its current section on this page and appears in the next column on the Manage board.

<Callout type="info">
  Thresholds are configured by your system administrator in the backend environment variables. Contact your clerk if you are unsure what values are currently set.
</Callout>

## Error States

| Error | Cause |
|-------|-------|
| Already Voted | Your account already has a vote on record for this proposal at this stage. Votes cannot be changed from the UI. |
| Not Authorized | Your account does not hold an eligible calling for this stage. |
| Stage Changed | The proposal moved to a new stage between when you opened the dialog and when you clicked a button. Close the dialog, refresh the page, and check the current stage. |
| Proposal Not Found | The proposal was deleted before you submitted your vote. Refresh the page. |
